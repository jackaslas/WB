Main Article

This is the main content of the article. Semantic HTML elements help structure your webpage in a meaningful way. This makes it easier for search engines to parse and for screen readers to navigate.

The main content typically goes inside a <article> element. Using proper semantics improves accessibility and SEO performance.

Sub Article

Another piece of content can be placed in a different article. This can be part of the main story or a related story.

Related Information

The <aside> element is used for content that is related to the main article but not part of it. This can include advertisements, related links, or extra information.
